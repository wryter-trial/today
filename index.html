The questions our writers ask the most

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions from our users.

How much content I can generate?

The amount of content you can generate depends on the plan you choose. We offer three plans: Free, Astrid Lindgren, and Shakespeare. The Free plan allows you to generate up to 10,000 words per month, while the Astrid Lindgren plan offers 30,000 words per month, and the Shakespeare plan offers unlimited words.

What languages does it support?

Our product supports a variety of languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, and Japanese, among others. You can select the language you want to use when generating your content.

How much does it cost?

We offer three plans: Free, Astrid Lindgren, and Shakespeare. The Free plan is completely free, while the Astrid Lindgren plan costs $9 per month, and the Shakespeare plan costs $29 per month.

Can I get a demo of the product?

Yes, you can! We offer a demo of our product so you can see how it works before you commit to a plan. To request a demo, please contact us via our website or email.
